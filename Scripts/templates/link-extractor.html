About:

This Link Extractor script will extract all internal and external links which will help with website analysis and search engine optimization. This link Extractor will help extract all links in a website and users can easily identify which links are useful or harmful and remove them if neccessary to prevent unecessary trouble.

Impact:

Having internal and external links in a website can lead to manipulative linking. These can lead to link penalties where some sites can get banned or removed from popular search engines. Manipulative linking is where websites make use of external or internal links to purposefully boost their website's ranking without proper usage of the links. Popular search engines like google, will deem this as manipulative linking and ban the website and this will affect the company or organisation's business or reputation.
